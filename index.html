The Kyn Project is a series of experiments
leveraging technology for the greater good.

We're a merry band of product-minded geeks
seeking to modernize the charity stack.

  • Discover

The problem (v0):
It's too hard to give your money away effectively

We started by building prototypes to test assumptions around a social network for charitably-minded individuals. The idea: if you're trying to assess a nonprofit, why not just copy someone else's homework?

Early Lessons Learned:
Why do all these APIs suck?

Payments: We're used to creating a Stripe account for online payments and integrating it in an afternoon. In the nonprofit world, the incumbent provider requires you to talk to a human first, then you pay a five figure 'integration' fee, then you pay $12k/yr in license fees, and THEN you get the honor of paying 25% more in transaction fees than Stripe.

There must be a better way.

Nonprofit Data: Why can we access a free API to pull over 3.4 million movie titles and poster images, yet if we want data on any of the 1.4 million nonprofits in the US it's stuck behind a paywall?

There must be a better way.

In early 2015 we shifted our focus away from the social network and towards new tests targeting improvements in the underlying APIs and infrastructure powering the charitable world.
